WHY The Rec Center Crossfit?

The first step is just getting in the door! So take a look at the schedule, pick a time that works for you, lace up your sneakers and get here! The first class is free so you have NOTHING to lose! We have a staff of amazing coaches that will guide you every step of the way to ensure you are confident and safe in what you are you doing. If you still feel like you may need to put in a little one on one time with a coach before you jump in, just email us and we will get that set up for you!

Your First Class is FREE...but we still need a couple things from you first:

1) Please visit this site and complete the step by step instructions on the form. CLICK HERE

2) Upon completing the online form, you will be redirected to a new screen. On the left side of the screen there is a button "Free Trial". Click that button, wait for the Green "OK" circle.

3) Find a class that works for your schedule

4) Come a couple minutes early, sign in to your class by typing your name on the iPad on the front desk,

5) Have fun!>
